brain of dane : bitcoinify me

Today, I embark on a small journey through the world of bitcoin. I'm not putting a whole lot of stock in it yet. I see it purely as an experimental hobby.

I have been studying up on bitcoin and crypto-currencies for the past few weeks and I think I'm ready to dip my toes in as a casual user.

The problem I'm facing is that I don't really want to put any investment money into it, which means I'm starting at 0.00BTC and essentially asking for handouts. Nobody likes asking for handouts.

The plan so far:

  1. Get some bitcoin
  2. Use my new bitcoin(s) to sell some personal items on BitMit
  3. Open a shop selling homemade geek items in small quantities Etsy style (sshhh... that part is secret for now)

I've resorted to using some of the anonymous click-farms to earn some money like BitVisitor. It feels a little dirty, but if it works... I think. I haven't actually received any bitcoin yet. I'll hopefully accumulate enough coin to post some personal items on BitMit soon.

The nice thing about BitMit is I can sell stuff ala eBay/Craigslist and fuel my internal minimalist gremlin while gaining bitcoins. Purge, purge, purge!
